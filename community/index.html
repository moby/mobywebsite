Community

The Moby project is composed of a library of components: runC/ containerd, LinuxKit, Infrakit, SwarmKit, HyperKit, DataKit, VPNKit, Notary, libnetwork, etc. All these components are developed in the open for the benefit of the greater Open Source community.
